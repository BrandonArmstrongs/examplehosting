Hi! Im Brandon, an 8th grader who really likes coding. I was using Google Classroom, but I kept thinking, “Why doesnt this have dark mode?” A lot of it felt confusing or hard to use. So I started building my own version—something simpler, cleaner, and more helpful. Thats how AltClass was born. It helps students keep track of missing work easily, looks way better (yes, it has dark mode!), and makes things easier for teachers too. I originally built it just for fun—but now its available for you to use.
With AltClass, students stay organized and engaged—making your job easier. Features like dark mode help reduce screen fatigue, while a clear view of missing assignments empowers students to take responsibility for their learning. The intuitive design means less time spent figuring things out and more time focused on what matters. And with responsive support just a click away, you can trust that both you and your students have what you need to succeed. AltClass keeps students on track—so you can focus on teaching, not troubleshooting.
